PROFILE PHOTO INSTRUCTIONS

Please rename your professional photo to "profile-photo.jpg" and place it in this directory.

The photo should be:
- Professional quality
- Clear headshot or upper body shot
- Square aspect ratio (1:1)
- Minimum size of 500x500 pixels
- JPG format
- Well-lit with a neutral background

This photo will appear in two places on your portfolio website:
1. The header/hero section at the top of the page
2. The About Me section

If your photo is in a different format (PNG, etc.), make sure to convert it to JPG and name it "profile-photo.jpg".
